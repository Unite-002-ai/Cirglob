# Cirglob Applicant Data Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

---

## 1. Overview

This Applicant Data Policy explains how Cirglob collects, processes, stores, and uses information submitted by candidates applying for roles across the Cirglob ecosystem.

By submitting an application, you acknowledge and agree to the terms outlined below.

---

## 2. What We Collect

When you apply to Cirglob, we may collect the following information:

### Personal Information
- Full name
- Email address
- Location
- LinkedIn profile
- Portfolio or personal website

### Professional Information
- CV / Resume
- GitHub or technical repositories
- Work history and experience
- Skills and technical background

### Application Content
- Motivation statements (“Why Cirglob?”)
- Cover letters or written responses
- Project descriptions or case studies

### Technical Data
- Device type
- Browser information
- IP-based location (for security and analytics)

---

## 3. How We Use Your Data

We use applicant data strictly for purposes related to:

- Evaluating job applications
- Matching candidates with relevant roles
- Assessing technical and cultural fit
- Improving hiring processes and workflows
- Communicating regarding application status

Cirglob does not sell applicant data.

---

## 4. AI-Assisted Evaluation

Cirglob may use AI systems to assist in:

- Resume parsing and structuring
- Skill classification
- Role matching recommendations
- Application triage (early-stage filtering)

However:

- Final hiring decisions are always made by humans
- AI systems are advisory, not authoritative

---

## 5. Data Sharing

Applicant data may be shared internally with:

- Founding team members
- Hiring managers
- Technical reviewers
- Selected advisors or mentors within the Cirglob ecosystem

We do not share your data with external advertisers or unrelated third parties.

---

## 6. Data Retention

We retain applicant data for:

- Active hiring cycles
- Future role consideration
- Internal talent mapping and benchmarking

You may request deletion of your data at any time.

---

## 7. Data Security

Cirglob applies reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect your data, including:

- Encrypted storage systems
- Access-controlled internal tools
- Limited role-based access to applicant data

No system is fully immune to risk, but we actively minimize exposure.

---

## 8. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

- Access your stored data
- Request corrections
- Request deletion
- Withdraw application consent

To exercise these rights, contact:

careers@cirglob.com

---

## 9. Global Compliance

Cirglob operates globally and aims to comply with applicable data protection laws, including:

- UK GDPR
- EU GDPR (where applicable)
- Other regional data protection frameworks

---

## 10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically.

Continued use of the application system after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
