# Cirglob Terms of Employment

Last Updated: April 2026

---

## 1. Overview

These Terms of Employment govern all individuals applying to, engaging with, or joining Cirglob in any capacity, including full-time, part-time, contract, advisory, or internship roles.

Cirglob operates as a venture builder and AI-native systems company. Employment is based on alignment, performance, and contribution to mission—not traditional corporate hierarchy.

---

## 2. Nature of Engagement

Employment or engagement with Cirglob is:

- At-will unless otherwise specified in a written agreement  
- Performance and mission aligned  
- Subject to ongoing evaluation of impact and contribution  

Cirglob reserves the right to modify, pause, or terminate engagements based on operational needs, performance, or strategic direction.

---

## 3. Compensation Philosophy

Cirglob does not follow rigid corporate compensation structures.

Instead, compensation may include:

- Base salary (role-dependent)
- Equity participation (select roles)
- Performance-based bonuses
- Tokenized or venture-linked incentives (where applicable)

All compensation details are defined in individual offer letters.

---

## 4. Intellectual Property (IP)

All work produced during engagement with Cirglob, including but not limited to:

- Code
- Research
- Models
- Systems
- Documentation
- Product designs

is the exclusive property of Cirglob unless explicitly agreed otherwise in writing.

Contributors may be credited internally or externally at Cirglob’s discretion.

---

## 5. Confidentiality

Employees and contributors must not disclose:

- Internal systems or architecture
- Investor or founder data
- Deal flow or pipeline information
- Product roadmaps or unreleased features
- Proprietary AI systems or infrastructure

Confidentiality obligations remain in effect after termination.

---

## 6. AI-Native Work Environment

Cirglob operates as an AI-first organization.

Employees are expected to:

- Use AI tools as part of daily workflows
- Optimize for automation and leverage systems thinking
- Build with AI augmentation, not manual repetition
- Continuously improve operational efficiency through tooling

Resistance to AI-native workflows may be considered misalignment with role expectations.

---

## 7. Conduct Expectations

All team members are expected to:

- Operate with extreme ownership
- Act like founders, not employees
- Maintain high integrity in decision-making
- Communicate clearly and asynchronously where possible
- Respect global, distributed collaboration norms

Cirglob maintains a high-performance culture with low tolerance for non-alignment.

---

## 8. Termination

Engagement may be terminated:

- With notice or immediately depending on severity or contract type
- Due to performance misalignment
- Due to violation of confidentiality or conduct expectations
- Due to strategic restructuring

---

## 9. Equity & Participation

Where applicable, equity or participation grants:

- Are subject to vesting schedules
- Require continued contribution
- May be modified in restructuring events
- Do not guarantee future valuation or liquidity

---

## 10. Global Operations

Cirglob operates globally.

Employees may work across jurisdictions, and must comply with:

- Local labor laws
- Tax obligations
- Work authorization requirements

Cirglob does not guarantee relocation or visa sponsorship unless explicitly stated.

---

## 11. Amendments

Cirglob may update these Terms at any time.

Continued engagement constitutes acceptance of updated terms.
