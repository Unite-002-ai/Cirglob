# Cirglob Careers Privacy Policy

_Last updated: April 2026_

---

## 1. Overview

This Careers Privacy Policy explains how Cirglob collects, processes, and uses personal data submitted through its careers and hiring system.

By applying for a role at Cirglob, you agree to the practices described in this document.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

When you apply for a role at Cirglob, we may collect the following information:

- Full name
- Email address
- Location
- LinkedIn profile
- Resume / CV
- Portfolio or GitHub links
- Application responses (e.g. motivation, experience, role fit)
- Any additional materials voluntarily submitted

We may also collect technical data such as:

- Device and browser information
- IP address
- Usage patterns within the careers portal

---

## 3. How We Use Your Data

We use applicant data strictly for hiring and evaluation purposes, including:

- Reviewing applications for open roles
- Assessing candidate fit for Cirglob and partner ecosystem opportunities
- Communicating with applicants regarding hiring stages
- Improving the hiring and application experience
- Internal talent analysis and role matching

We do **not** sell applicant data.

---

## 4. Data Sharing

Your data may be shared internally within Cirglob and with:

- Hiring team members
- Technical reviewers and interviewers
- Founders or partners within the Cirglob ecosystem (when relevant to role evaluation)

All shared access is limited to hiring-related purposes only.

---

## 5. Data Retention

We retain applicant data for as long as necessary to:

- Evaluate your application
- Consider you for future opportunities
- Maintain hiring records

You may request deletion of your data at any time (see Section 7).

---

## 6. Security

We apply reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data against:

- Unauthorized access
- Alteration
- Disclosure
- Loss or misuse

However, no system is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

---

## 7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

- Access your personal data
- Request correction of inaccurate data
- Request deletion of your data
- Withdraw your application or consent

Requests can be made at:

📩 legal@cirglob.com

---

## 8. International Applicants

Cirglob operates globally. Your data may be processed outside your country of residence, including jurisdictions with different data protection laws.

---

## 9. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party tools for:

- Application processing
- Resume storage
- Analytics

These providers are required to handle data securely and only for intended purposes.

---

## 10. Changes to This Policy

Cirglob may update this Careers Privacy Policy periodically.

Continued use of the careers system constitutes acceptance of any updates.

---

## 11. Contact

For all privacy-related inquiries:
